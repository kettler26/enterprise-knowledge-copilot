# Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Use this script to qualify SaaS leads for a paid pilot.

## 0-3 min: Context and Objective

Opening:

- "Goal for today: understand your support workflow, baseline metrics, and whether a 6-week pilot can produce measurable ROI."

Alignment question:

- "If this pilot works, what business outcome matters most to you: faster responses, lower handling time, or more deflection?"

## 3-10 min: Current State Mapping

Questions:

- "What is your monthly ticket volume and seasonal variance?"
- "Which channels dominate volume: email, chat, in-app, social?"
- "What tools do agents use today (Zendesk/Intercom, Notion/Confluence, Slack)?"
- "Where do answers break down most often?"
- "How long does onboarding take before new reps are fully productive?"

## 10-18 min: Metrics and Economics

Questions:

- "What are your current AHT and first response time by queue?"
- "What percentage of tickets are repetitive?"
- "What is your loaded support cost per hour?"
- "What is your current escalation rate?"

Qualification:

- If no baseline metrics exist, propose week-1 KPI baseline as part of pilot.

## 18-24 min: Pilot Fit and Constraints

Questions:

- "Which integration can we start with in a read-only mode?"
- "Any security constraints: SSO, data residency, audit requirements?"
- "Who will be business owner and technical champion?"
- "Can your team join a weekly 45-minute KPI review?"

Go/No-Go checks:

- clear owner
- access to at least one knowledge source
- willingness to track KPI deltas

## 24-28 min: Pilot Proposal

Offer template:

- 6-week paid pilot
- 1 ticketing + 1 documentation source
- baseline + weekly KPI dashboard
- final ROI readout and annual rollout plan

Commercial framing:

- "We define success upfront with measurable thresholds so conversion is objective."

## 28-30 min: Close and Next Steps

Close questions:

- "Are you open to a paid pilot starting in the next 2-4 weeks?"
- "Who needs to sign off commercially and technically?"

Next actions:

- send SOW draft within 24 hours
- request technical checklist and credentials
- book kickoff and weekly review slots

## Red Flags (Disqualify or Delay)

- no data access path
- no owner/champion assigned
- no willingness to run KPI baseline
- expects fully autonomous writes on day 1
